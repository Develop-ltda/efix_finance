Structural Advantages
⚡
D+0 Redemption — Core Moat
Same-day redemption of DI fund shares at full NAV eliminates FX hedge costs (~2–3% annually). Serves as the liquidation guarantee for Morpho positions. No crypto collateral can match this: DI fund is backed by Brazilian sovereign debt, not market price.
🏛️
Regulatory Infrastructure
CVM Act 23.635/2025 under Resolution 88/2022. HausBank provides PIX infrastructure (Central Bank regulated). Genial IAAS for DI fund custody. Institutional-grade regulatory stack — not a DeFi workaround.
📐
Yield Architecture
CDI ~14.9% APY base. Leverage via Morpho Blue at 50% LTV, USDC borrow at ~5%. Target 25–35% net APY. Spread widens as CDI rises. No impermanent loss, no liquidation via oracle manipulation — liquidation is fund redemption.
