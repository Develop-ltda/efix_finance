Why a full floor
LATITUDE is the first pool in the BTR catalog backed by an entire floor — not scattered units. That structural choice unlocks two effects that traditional fractional real-estate doesn't offer: internal diversification of cashflow, and operational efficiency from concentration.
01 · Diversification
6 cashflow streams, 1 token
Each studio runs an independent STR rental cycle. Stays, occupancy, daily rate and seasonal demand fluctuate idiosyncratically. With 6 partially correlated streams, the pool's monthly distribution variance drops — an attribute institutional allocators value when sizing positions. A vacancy in 1 studio reduces income by ~17%, not 100% as it would in a single-unit pool.
−40% expected variance reduction vs single-unit pool
02 · Operational efficiency
1 floor = 1 housekeeping route
All 6 studios on the same floor share the same housekeeping cycle, check-in desk, laundry trip, supply restock, and maintenance call-out. The operator's marginal cost per studio drops vs scattered units. Rule of thumb: 8-12% lower opex per studio when 6 units share a floor.

Risk & Compliance
Read before you allocate
LATITUDE is a Regulation S Category 3 offering. Primary distribution is restricted: no U.S. persons may participate in the initial offering. EFIX Plataforma de Tokenização e Crowdfunding Ltda is the issuer; Lobie operates the STR floor post-habite-se; Harris & Trotter audits cashflow observations before they land on-chain. LATITUDE tokens will trade freely on global secondary markets after the 12-month Reg S compliance period.

Key risks

Long construction risk: habite-se forecast 2028/2029 — ~25-37 months from offering close. Operational phase cash flows depend on habite-se being issued on schedule. During any delay, CDI-proxy yield continues at ~14.4% a.a.

STR demand risk: operational rent depends on Centro Rio short-term-rental demand, which is sensitive to macro and tourism factors. Reviver Centro program revival has supported demand in recent years.

Planta-to-habite-se valuation risk: the 25-40% appreciation curve is a base case. Centro Rio market dynamics, regulatory changes, or construction delays could compress or extend it.

Regulatory risk: CVM, BCB, or securities-law changes in Brazil or Reg S compliance changes in the U.S. could alter the offering's structure.

Smart-contract risk: all contracts are immutable and audited; however, no smart contract is risk-free.

When does the offering open?
LATITUDE primary opens in November 2026 — three months after the building's planta launch in August 2026. Reserve your slot now via the form above.
Why "tokenize at launch" matters?
Traditional REITs and FIIs only buy stabilized assets — leaving the planta-to-habite-se appreciation curve (~25-40% in Rio Centro) entirely to incorporators. LATITUDE tokenizes at the moment of building launch, so retail token-holders capture that curve too. It's a profile that doesn't exist elsewhere in the public market.
Why a full floor instead of scattered units?
Two reasons. (1) Internal diversification: 6 cashflows partially decorrelate, lowering monthly distribution variance. (2) Operational efficiency: 1 floor = 1 housekeeping route, 1 check-in desk, 1 laundry trip — ~8-12% lower opex per studio than scattered units. Both effects are accretive to net yield.
What's the minimum allocation?
R$ 1,000 (or ~$200 USD equivalent). No maximum for primary.
Can U.S. persons buy LATITUDE?
Not in the primary offering (Reg S Category 3). U.S. persons can only purchase on the secondary market (Uniswap V3) after the 12-month distribution compliance period ends.
Can I trade LATITUDE on global exchanges?
Yes. After offering close, LATITUDE is freely tradeable on Uniswap V3 (Base) and listed on CoinGecko, CoinMarketCap and DefiLlama. Any exchange that supports Base ERC-20 tokens can list LATITUDE. Reg S Cat 3 imposes a 12-month restriction on U.S.-person purchases only.
How are dividends paid during the long construction phase?
In BRLE (the BRL stablecoin on Base), monthly. CDI-proxy ~14.4% a.a. throughout construction (~25-37 months). The operator calls depositDividend() monthly. You call claim() from your wallet to pull your accrued BRLE.
How does LATITUDE differ from SALRIO and FIRST?
All three are Hybrid framework BTR pools on Base. SALRIO is 5 studios in Porto Maravilha (R$ 1.86M, ~21% IRR). FIRST is 1 studio in Humaitá (R$ 680k, ~22% IRR). LATITUDE is 1 full floor (6 studios, R$ 4.05M, ~21% IRR) with the unique "tokenize at launch" feature for capturing planta-to-habite-se appreciation. LATITUDE is the largest, longest-duration, and most internally-diversified pool.
