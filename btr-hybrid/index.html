How it works
From offering to rent, in four steps.
The Hybrid contract gives investors BRLE distributions from day one. During construction those distributions come from CDI-linked yield; once the building is operational, the same contract routes real rental cashflow instead. One ERC-20, four lifecycle phases tagged on-chain.
Step 01
Subscribe
Buy at par
Investors subscribe at par from the operator Safe treasury or buy on the Uniswap V3 pool. Reg S Category 3 offering.
Step 02
Hold
Track the pool
Balance entitles holder to pro-rata cashflow. ERC-20, ERC-2612 Permit, freely transferable, fully composable.
Step 03
Collect
Claim BRLE
Operator Safe calls depositDividend() monthly. Anyone calls claim() to pull accrued BRLE. Phase-tagged on-chain (CDI proxy vs rental).
Step 04
Unlock
Borrow or swap
Deposit the share as collateral on Morpho Blue, borrow USDC up to 62.5% LTV. Or sell into the Uniswap pool for instant liquidity.
Lifecycle
Four phases, one contract, zero upgrades
Cashflows shift across distinct phases — each tagged on-chain so the audit trail distinguishes pre-operational yield (BRLE-linked CDI proxy) from real rental income once habite-se is issued.
Phase 0
Construction
CDI proxy
Operator parks IPO BRL in BRLE-linked yield. Monthly CDI yield (~14.4% a.a.) distributes as dividendPhase = 0 (ConstructionProxy).
Phase 1
Ramp-up
Mixed sources
Partial habite-se issued. Units come online progressively. dividendPhase = 1 (RampUp). Both CDI and rental cashflow.
Phase 2
Operational
Rent pro-rata
All units stabilized. dividendPhase = 2 (Operational). Straight rental cashflow distributed monthly via Batog 2018 math.
Any time
Extraordinary
Non-recurring
Capex refunds, legal settlements, insurance payouts. dividendPhase = 3 (ExtraordinaryDist). Same math, different tag.
The Unlock

Features
Primitives only. No clever abstractions.
Every piece is a well-understood building block: Batog 2018 dividend math, OpenZeppelin v5.4 AccessControl, Gordon Growth formula for on-chain NPV, Chainlink for BRL/USD, Morpho Blue's immutable-market primitive. Integration reviewers recognize every line.
O(1) Math
Scalable reward distribution
Single dividendPerShareScaled counter, lazy settlement on transfer. O(1) deposit, O(1) claim, zero off-chain infra. Published Batog 2018 pattern with a decade of production use.
NFT-Backed
Real cashflow backing
Lobie Unit NFTs registered via BTROfferingRegistry link each share to specific rental assets. Each NFT carries audited monthly observations once habite-se fires.
Gordon Growth
NPV oracle on-chain
Registry exposes gordonGrowthNPV(dividendPerPeriod, r, g) as a pure function. Any frontend or agent can reprice the share at custom discount / growth assumptions without off-chain models.
Reg S Cat 3
Offshore securities framing
Wyoming LLC SPE structure (BTS engagement), Regulation S Category 3 with 12-month distribution compliance period. Legend embedded in ERC-20 token name. No U.S. persons in primary.
Morpho
Immutable lending market
Hybrid-share / USDC market on Morpho Blue with 62.5% LLTV, par-peg × Chainlink BRL/USD oracle, Adaptive IRM. Isolated, permissionless, unupgradeable — standard Morpho primitive.
Phase Tags
On-chain audit trail
Every depositDividend is tagged with its lifecycle phase (Construction / RampUp / Operational / Extraordinary). Holders, auditors, and agents can attribute yield to its true source.
