How it works
One share. One studio. One monthly dividend.
Each SALRIO token represents 1 ⁄ 1,861,310 of the pool — 1 SALRIO = R$ 1.00 at par. Lobie operates the studios; the SPE collects rent; the Safe deposits BRLE dividends monthly. The contract does the rest via Batog 2018 math (O(1) per account, no merkle trees, no off-chain indexers).

Lifecycle
Four phases, one contract, zero upgrades
Sal Rio is pre-operational at IPO. Cashflows shift across distinct phases — each tagged on-chain so the audit trail distinguishes pre-operational yield (sBRLE / CDI proxy) from actual rental income once habite-se is issued.
Month 1 – 18
Construction
~R$ 22.3k / month
Lobie parks IPO BRL in sBRLE. Monthly CDI yield (~14.4% a.a.) distributes as dividendPhase = 0 (ConstructionProxy).
Month 19 – 24
Ramp-up
Mixed sources
Partial habite-se issued. Studios come online progressively. dividendPhase = 1 (RampUp). Both CDI and rental cashflow.
Month 25+
Operational
~R$ 13k / month
All 5 studios stabilized at ~R$ 2.6k net payout each. dividendPhase = 2 (Operational). Straight rental cashflow.
Any time
Extraordinary
Non-recurring
Capex refunds, legal settlements, insurance payouts. dividendPhase = 3 (ExtraordinaryDist). Same math, different tag.
The Unlock
Brazilian real estate credit, meet DeFi rates

Features
Primitives only. No clever abstractions.

O(1) Math

NFT-Backed

5 Lobie Unit NFTs registered via BTROfferingRegistry link SALRIO to specific studios in the Lobie registry at 0xd8C32a41… Each NFT carries audited monthly observations once habite-se fires.
