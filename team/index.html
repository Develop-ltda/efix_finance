Ernesto Luiz Otero Neto

Chief Executive Officer — EFIX Securitizadora & HausBank
Economist with 15+ years across capital markets, structured finance, and fintech. Started his career at Goldman Sachs, then moved to investment banking in Brazil before founding a development company that was successfully sold in 2021. Designed and built the efixDI+ protocol end-to-end: from CVM regulatory architecture through smart contract deployment across Polygon and Base to DeFi integrations on Morpho Blue. Led the structuring of R$180MM+ in tokenized receivables with <0.5% default rates.
