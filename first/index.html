Risk & Compliance
Read before you allocate
FIRST is a Regulation S Category 3 offering. Primary distribution is restricted: no U.S. persons may participate in the initial offering. EFIX Plataforma de Tokenização e Crowdfunding Ltda is the issuer; Lobie is the operator; Harris & Trotter audits cashflow observations before they land on-chain. FIRST tokens will trade freely on global secondary markets after the 12-month Reg S compliance period.

Key risks

Construction risk: habite-se (target Jan/2027) may be delayed. Operational phase cash flows depend on habite-se being issued on schedule. During any delay, CDI-proxy yield continues at ~14.4% a.a.

STR demand risk: operational rent depends on Rio de Janeiro short-term-rental demand, which is seasonal and sensitive to macro and tourism factors. The Botafogo Privilege benchmark suggests 65-92% occupancy is achievable in this corridor.

Single-unit concentration: FIRST is backed by 1 studio. A vacancy or maintenance event affects the full pool's monthly distribution. Smaller pools amplify both upside and idiosyncratic risk.

Regulatory risk: CVM, BCB, or securities-law changes in Brazil or Reg S compliance changes in the U.S. could alter the offering's structure.

Smart-contract risk: all contracts are immutable and audited; however, no smart contract is risk-free.

Common questions

When does the offering open?
FIRST primary opens in May 2026. Reserve your slot now via the form above; tokens deliver to your smart wallet on offering close.
What's the minimum allocation?
R$ 1,000 (or ~$200 USD equivalent). No maximum for primary.
Can U.S. persons buy FIRST?
Not in the primary offering (Reg S Category 3). U.S. persons can only purchase on the secondary market (Uniswap V3) after the 12-month distribution compliance period ends.
Can I trade FIRST on global exchanges?
Yes. After offering close, FIRST is freely tradeable on Uniswap V3 (Base) and listed on CoinGecko, CoinMarketCap and DefiLlama. Any exchange that supports Base ERC-20 tokens can list FIRST. Reg S Cat 3 imposes a 12-month restriction on U.S.-person purchases only.
How are dividends paid?
In BRLE (the BRL stablecoin on Base). Operator calls depositDividend() monthly. You call claim() from your wallet to pull your accrued BRLE — or any third-party can call claimFor(you).
Can I borrow against FIRST?
Yes — once the Morpho Blue market opens, deposit FIRST as collateral and borrow USDC up to ~62.5% LTV. Also tradeable on Uniswap V3 if you prefer instant liquidity.
Is there a lock-up?
No contract-level lock-up. However Reg S Category 3 imposes a 12-month "distribution compliance period" during which transfers to U.S. persons are restricted.
How does FIRST differ from SALRIO?
Both are Hybrid framework BTR pools on Base. SALRIO is 5 studios in Porto Maravilha (R$ 1.86M); FIRST is 1 studio in Humaitá (R$ 680k) — same operator (Lobie), same dividend asset (BRLE), same legal framing (Reg S Cat 3). FIRST is the smaller, premium-Zona-Sul pool.
