The Opportunity
Porto Maravilha, reshaped by short-term rentals
Sal Rio Residences sits in Porto Maravilha — central Rio's reborn waterfront district, home to Museu do Amanhã, MAR, Pedra do Sal, Cais do Valongo, VLT and Santos Dumont airport. Purpose-built for short-term-rental operation by Lobie. SALRIO tokenizes fractional equity in 5 of its studios, so global investors can capture the rental economics of one of the world's most in-demand tourism cities — without Brazilian real-estate bureaucracy.

Porto Maravilha is Rio's fastest-growing tourism corridor: steps from Museu do Amanhã, Praça Mauá, MAR, Armazém 2, and the VLT-to-Ipanema line. Risk & Compliance
Read before you allocate
SALRIO is a Regulation S Category 3 offering. Primary distribution is restricted: no U.S. persons may participate in the initial offering. EFIX Securitizadora is the issuer; Lobie is the operator; Harris & Trotter audits cashflow observations before they land on-chain.

Key risks

Construction risk: habite-se may be delayed. Operational phase cash flows depend on habite-se being issued on schedule. During the delay, CDI-proxy yield continues.

STR demand risk: operational rent depends on Rio de Janeiro short-term-rental demand, which is seasonal and sensitive to macroeconomic and tourism factors.

Regulatory risk: CVM, BCB, or securities-law changes in Brazil or Reg S compliance-period changes in the U.S. could alter the offering's structure.

Smart-contract risk: all contracts are immutable and audited; however, no smart contract is risk-free.

FAQ
Common questions
If you don't see your question here, email ernesto.otero@hausbank.com.br.
How long does the allocation take?
PIX: SALRIO tokens are usually delivered within 1 hour of your deposit clearing. ACH: 1-2 business days. International Wire: same-day if sent before 15:00 UTC.
What's the minimum allocation?
R$ 1,000 (or ~$200 USD equivalent). No maximum for primary.
Can U.S. persons buy SALRIO?
Not in the primary offering (Reg S Category 3). U.S. persons can only purchase on the secondary market (Uniswap V3) after the 12-month distribution compliance period ends.
How are dividends paid?
In BRLE (the BRL stablecoin on Base). Operator calls depositDividend() monthly. You call claim() from your wallet to pull your accrued BRLE — or any third-party can call claimFor(you).
Can I borrow against SALRIO?
Yes — deposit SALRIO as collateral on Morpho Blue and borrow USDC up to 62.5% LTV. Also tradeable on Uniswap V3 if you prefer instant liquidity.
Is there a lock-up?
No contract-level lock-up. However Reg S Category 3 imposes a 12-month "distribution compliance period" during which transfers outside the offshore community are restricted.
